Low calorie Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Did you ever wonder if there was a way to eat the foods that you loves witout worrying about the calories. Well Good news! There ia a lot of recipes by just making a few swaps and changes, you can enjoy the foods that you love without feeling guilty.

For two portions

Ingredients

  • Chickpea Pasta
  • Chicken Breast
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Almond Milk
  • Spices:
    • Onion powder
    • Garlic Powder
    • Salt
    • Paprika
    • Italian Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Make the chicken: Take one chicken breast and cut it in smal pieces. Add the spices to it and mix everything in a bowl. Adjust the stove at medium heat. When the pan is already hot, add a square of butter. Add the chicken to the pan. Cooking it should be take between 15 to 20 minutes.After that,leave the chiken in the pan in very low heat
  2. Make the sauce: Add in a Blender 1 ¼ of cottage cheese and ¼ of almond milk. Add one spoon of garlic powder, onion powder, italian seasoning, and a pinch of salt. Lastly add ¼ of parmesan cheese. Blend everything together until you have a creamy sauce.
  3. Boil a pot with water
  4. When it's already boiling, add some salt, and add the pasta
  5. After the pasta is done, in the pan that you leave the chiken in low heat, add the sauce and one or two spoon of pasta water.
  6. Drain the pasta water.
  7. Mix the chicken with the sauce, then add the pasta and mix everything together.
  8. Put everything in aplate, add some parmesan cheese if you like, and ENJOY IT!.
